Funded Programs

Government-Funded Care Options

Many Alberta families qualify for funded home care programs. We help you navigate the process from application to approval.

Client Directed Home Care

CDHCI — AHS Funded through Alberta Blue Cross

Eligible Albertans can receive home care services at no direct cost through the Client Directed Home Care Insurance program. AHS determines eligibility and funding levels, and we handle the rest — from caregiver matching to ongoing care management.

Veterans Independence Program

VIP — Veterans Affairs Canada

Supporting Canadian veterans with in-home care coverage through Veterans Affairs Canada. The VIP program helps veterans maintain their independence at home with funded personal care, housekeeping, and grounds maintenance services.
